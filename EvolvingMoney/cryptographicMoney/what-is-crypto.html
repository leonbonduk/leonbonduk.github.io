E-Money


One of the most exciting and least understood areas in finance is that which relates to online purchasing and online money. Commerce has moved online, and those that have made e-commerce "frictionless" (Paypal, Amazon) have made benefitted greatly. It hasn't stopped with Amazon and Paypal, innovations continue. Payments systems like transferwise compete with western union, and even uber and airbnb can is some sense be considerred as frictionless payments systems
